We are three generations of Hugh that simply love to brew

Hugh I

Good day. I am the original Hugh and the original brewer of the Moloney clan. With the help of my own father, Tom Moloney, and his father's wheat farm in county Kildare, I began brewing beer in the 1970s. Since then alot has changed, but excellent taste of my delicious beer hasn't.

Hugh II

Growing up I brewed beers with my father and drank very little else. However as a student of classics and ancient literature, I frequently travelled to the mediteranian. There, I discovered a love of wine and a passion for the farming culture that ensures our grapes recieve the most love and care possible.

Hugh III

Hi there! I'm the youngest Hugh and in charge of the mead flavourings. I'm passionate about high-quality honey, cinnamin sticks and protecting our wildflowers. My favourite mead of ours is our Wild Hot Mead, as it keeps me toasty on a cold night!
