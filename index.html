THE HUGHS

our story

We are three generations of Hughs who simply love to brew! Hugh I began home-brewing in 1983 with his own father Tom Moloney, and the wheat from their family farm in county Kildare. Hugh II and Hugh III learned the trade and began to experiment with wine and mead, and in 2017 Hugh's Brews was founded. From the humble beginings in 1983 alot has changed, but excellent taste of the Moloney brews hasn't.
