Mead, nectar of the Gods

Referred to as “nectar of the gods” by ancient Greeks, mead was believed to be dew sent from the heavens and collected by bees. While our mead may not be sent from gods, it certainly is heavenly!

Hugh's Happy Hoppy Beers

Made from the finest sun-soaked, rain-kissed barley grown on the Moloney farm fields of Kildare, our beers are simply the most happy, hoppy brews in Ireland!

Wine born in a windy storm

Using the best grape-juice the medetaranian has to offer, we age our wine in barrels made from lightening-struck wood, bent lovingly into shape over one hundred years ago.
